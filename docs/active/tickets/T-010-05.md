## Context

V100 will be tested on a real phone. Current issues: no haptic feedback, figure card tap targets may be too small (Figma shows 120×145px), lens grid scroll may not have momentum, theme bar tabs may be hard to tap quickly. Figma sticky notes: "it could get up to 90 sec to load: between the screens need animation typing starts → UI".

## Acceptance Criteria

- `research.md` documents current tap target sizes and scroll behaviour
- `design.md` specifies haptic pattern per interaction type (light / medium / heavy)
- Implementation: `navigator.vibrate()` on figure card tap, theme switch, and CTA buttons
- Implementation: lens grid has `-webkit-overflow-scrolling: touch` and proper padding-bottom
- Implementation: all interactive elements meet 44×44px minimum tap target
- Implementation: theme tab bar has enough height and padding for reliable tapping
- Implementation: loading message cycles with typewriter feel per Figma note

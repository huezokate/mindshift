## Vision

Full screen + UX rebuild of V100 based on the updated primary user flow (Figma 200:2865). Two parallel tracks:

1. **New screens & UI** — re-architect the lens selection into a horizontal carousel inside the preview popup, replace the tab bar with a scrollable pill selector, redesign account form, add share preview + post-share success popup stubs.

2. **CTA copy research** — audit all button/label/heading copy against UX research on emotional apps, implement improved wording across all themes.

### What changed in the Figma flow

- **Theme selector**: horizontal infinite scroll with highlighted pill, not a fixed 3-tab bar
- **Lens popup redesign**: lens avatar + quote + bio + horizontal carousel strip at the bottom to swipe between all lenses (replaces separate grid screen)
- **Lens limit**: 3 per problem per day (resets midnight), not 3 per session
- **Account form**: nickname + email + confirm email + privacy note (remove phone/password)
- **Share preview**: full screen with watermark + social icons + "minimize watermark" upsell
- **Post-share popup**: "Great — keep it in your journal" with account CTA, auto-closes 5s
- **Mind-mapping CTA**: secondary CTA on landing (already present, keep)

### Tiers (from sticky notes)
- Free: 3 lenses / 1 problem / day — large watermark
- Account: 5 lenses / 3 problems / day — small watermark + journal
- Paid: $1.99 / 24h unlimited or $8.99 / 30 days premium
